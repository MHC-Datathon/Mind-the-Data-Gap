Welcome!

Our project focuses on ACE (Automated Camera Enforcement) violations along the M101 bus route! In this study, we dive deep into the data to explore the top repeat offenders for exempt violations, highlight the key hotspots where and when these violations occur most frequently, and compare repeat exempt offenders with vehicles that received issued violations. Our goal is to provide a clear, data-driven look at patterns of enforcement and identify areas where violations are most common. This website is your hub to explore all of our findings, check out the tools and methods we used, and learn more about why we focused on the M101 route.
